About Tech Fest 2026

Tech Fest 2026 is a premier technology event organized by AVS Engineering College. The event brings together students, innovators, and technology enthusiasts to explore the latest trends in technology and innovation.

Objectives

  • Promote technical knowledge and innovation.
  • Encourage student participation in technology events.
  • Develop teamwork and problem-solving skills.
  • Provide networking opportunities.

Benefits

  • Learn emerging technologies.
  • Gain practical experience.
  • Interact with experts and peers.
  • Enhance career opportunities.
